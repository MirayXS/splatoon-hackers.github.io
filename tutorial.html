This is a Tutorial for Installing Splatoon 2 Hacks

Things You'll need:

1) A Hacked Switch.

2) Enough Space on your Micro SD card.

3) Latest Atmosphere (Click to Download)

Instructions:

1) Download latest Atmosphere Release and extract the zip into your SD's root, prepare fusee-primary.bin.

2) Download latest IPSwitch and put it to /switch folder in your SD(Click to Download)

3) Open /switch/ipswitch on your micro sd card and make any folder (Splatoon 2 for Example), and put your pchtxt to it.

4) Start your switch with fusee-primary and open homebrew launcher (hold L while opening album or hold R while opening any app), then find and open ipswitch.

5) Press Toggle Patch Contents and Choose what patches you want, Green = Enabled Red = Disabled.

6) Press "Generate" and press A, then close Homebrew and launch Splatoon 2.
